Course Description

In COMM 2034, we have covered many different aspects of visual media throughout the semester. Each portion of this class is broken into "modules" covering different topics. These have included: design, photo, video and building a web presence. All of these skills we be very important as we look to the future and take jobs in different sectors. All of these companies will likely expect us to have a working knowledge of different aspects of design and web presence. Overall, these skills are a good asset in general, as they can be used to great designs for family and friends.
