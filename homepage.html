Fall 2018

In the fall of 2018, I am taking four different classes at Virginia Tech. These have all been very challenging, but exciting. It has been a unique semester in that two of my classes are in business and two are communications which makes for a great contrast and no two days are the same! Also this semester I have worked in both the Virginia Tech Athletic Ticket Office and the Athletics Marketing Office. I have been covering volleyball, track and field, cross country, lacrosse and football for Marketing this year which has made for some incredible experiences both on and off the field. Moving forward, I cannot wait to see what the future holds and where next semester will take me.
