MKTG 3504

Professor Hsu

Course Description

MKTG 3504 covers several different aspects of advertising in today's world. This class is a diverse and intense one that provides many opportunities for growth and a deeper udnerstanding of what it takes to be a successful member of an advetising or marketing agency. Taking the time to know not only the numbers and process behind so many of the advertisements we see each day, but how they were developed and how to create your own successful campaigns is very important.

Teacher's Bio / Interests

Professor Hsu enjoys reading about new creative campaigns, different advertisements, and addressing issues relevant to marketers.

My Opinion

Our semester long campaign in this class has been very challenging, but a great way to learn how to be a successful advertiser. Taking the time to think critically and udnerstand how companies go about marketing to each of us is incredibly important.
